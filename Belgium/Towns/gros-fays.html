Gros-Fays



Gros-Fays is a village in the Belgian municipality of Bièvre, located in the province of Namur. Gros-Fays is part of the association that brings together the most beautiful villages in Wallonia.

The village is located in the Ardennes, on the heights of the valley of the Semois, a tributary of the Meuse. The villages that surround it are Alle-sur-Semois, Cornimont, Chairière, Mouzaive and Oizy. The village of Gros-Fays has a hamlet located about 1 kilometer to the north called Six-Planes.
